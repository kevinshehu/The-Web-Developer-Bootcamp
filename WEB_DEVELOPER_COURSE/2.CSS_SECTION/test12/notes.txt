1.  Inline elements: 
        -   fit in alongside other elements
        -   width & height are ignored
        -   margin & padding push elements away horizontally but not vertically


2.  Block level elements: 
        -   take up a whole block of space
        -   width, height, margin, padding are respected


3. Inline-Block:
        -   behaved like an inline element expect width, height, margin, padding are respected
